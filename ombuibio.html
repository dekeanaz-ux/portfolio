My Career

Since he entered the industry of electrical Engineering, Henry has been pushing forth new and innovative ideas. With over 2years of experience gathered in different organisations including Nairobi Metropolitans Services, Kenyatta University, Central Electrical international Ltd and Faina consultance. Henry specialized in Power Engineering, and possesses a strong Electrical Engineering background and understands quite well concepts behind Electrical Power Engineering. Henry graduated from kenyatta university Nairobi Kenya with bachelors degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. His university project, described a new concept of chicken farming in small scale farms where he developed a prototype capable of monitoring feeds quantity, automatically releases the feeds to the feeders and water to the drunkers at the appropriate set time. The prototype was also capable of sending SMS alerts to the user him/her the commencement of the feeding process and the remaining quantity of the feeds. Henry's possession of strong Arduino, EagleCAD PCB development ,AutoCAD Electrical, HTML, and CSS skills have seen him develop some noble prototypes e.g. Water leakage detector, Automated home security system, and smart rechargable portable power bank even though none has been published. Outside Work

Outside professional work, Henry has been engaging in agricultural activities. He possesses a strong understanding of chicken farming. During the pandemic period, He was able to raise 200 chicken at his mother's rural farm in Nyansiong'o, Nyamira county some of which were sold for profit. Henry loves the soil since he's the son of the soil. Having been born and raised in a rich Agricultural background, he simply understands the concepts. He knows traditional agriculture, nature's mechanisms and want to go deeper into them, he wants to study them more deeply in favour of man and for the preservation of nature itself. He is a modern man, aligned with his time but conscious of the value of tradition. He is a real farmer, who not only aims at preserving nature, but who has also found out an alternative and virtuous way to earn a decent quantity of money with his farming skills. He flanks old practices to new techniques and technologies, essential for real regenerative agriculture: crop rotations so as not to stress the soil.
